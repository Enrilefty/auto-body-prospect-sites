Terms and Conditions.

Customer authorization, estimates, payment, warranty, liability, dispute, and privacy terms for Onsite Auto service.

Welcome to Michael A. Smith LLC DBA ONSITE AUTO. These terms are intended to keep service expectations clear and professional. By engaging with Onsite Auto’s services, customers agree to the terms specified here.

1. Service Authorization

  • Customers must provide written or verbal authorization before repair work begins.
  • Major repairs or services exceeding $500 may require a signed work order.
  • If additional repairs are needed beyond the original scope, Onsite Auto will contact the customer for approval before proceeding.

2. Estimates and Pricing

  • Estimates are approximations based on initial diagnostics. Actual costs may vary depending on the complexity of the issue.
  • Estimates are valid for 30 days from the date issued.
  • Pricing may change due to parts availability or additional required work. Customers will be notified of adjustments.

3. Payment Terms

  • Full payment is due immediately following completion of services unless otherwise agreed upon in writing by management.
  • Accepted payment methods include cash, credit/debit cards, and digital payments such as Apple Pay and Google Pay.
  • The vehicle and keys will not be released until the balance is paid in full or an approved payment arrangement is made.
  • Partial payment may be required before work begins to obtain parts, up to but not exceeding 60% of the total invoice.
  • A 5% late fee applies to unpaid balances after 30 days from the invoice date.

4. Parts and Warranties

  • Onsite Auto uses high-quality OEM or equivalent parts unless otherwise requested by the customer.
  • Parts warranties are subject to manufacturer terms and conditions.
  • Labor warranties are valid for 12 months or 12,000 miles, whichever comes first, covering only defects directly associated with work performed by Onsite Auto technicians.
  • Onsite Auto does not warranty customer-provided parts and may refuse warranties at management discretion.
  • Warranties are void if the vehicle is serviced, repaired, modified, diagnosed, adjusted, inspected, or otherwise worked on by another repair facility, technician, or individual after service is complete without prior written approval from Onsite Auto.

5. Vehicle Drop-Off and Pick-Up

  • Customers are responsible for arranging vehicle drop-off and pick-up when applicable.
  • Vehicles left on the premises for more than 7 days after service completion may incur a daily storage fee of $25.
  • Onsite Auto is not liable for loss or damage to vehicles left beyond the agreed pick-up timeframe.

6. Liability and Insurance

  • Onsite Auto takes necessary precautions but is not liable for damages caused by unforeseen events such as natural disasters, theft, or vandalism while a vehicle is in its care.
  • Customers are responsible for ensuring their vehicle is adequately insured.
  • Onsite Auto cannot be held liable for damages or losses incurred during the course of work beyond applicable law and approved service terms.

7. Customer Responsibilities

  • Customers must disclose known vehicle issues before service.
  • Modifications or aftermarket parts must be disclosed to avoid complications during repairs.
  • Customers are responsible for removing valuable personal belongings before service; Onsite Auto is not liable for lost or stolen items.

8. Labor Charges and Issue Resolution

  • Customers are responsible for labor costs incurred during diagnostic and repair attempts, regardless of whether the initial issue is resolved.
  • Labor costs include diagnostic time, attempted repairs, and additional authorized work.
  • Refunds for unsatisfactory work apply only to portions of labor deemed incomplete or defective upon management review.

9. Cancellation and Refund Policy

  • Repair orders may be canceled before service begins without penalty.
  • Refunds for incomplete or unsatisfactory work are subject to management review.
  • If parts have already been ordered, the cost of those parts may be non-refundable.

10. Dispute Resolution

  • Disputes regarding services rendered must be submitted in writing within 14 days of service completion.
  • Onsite Auto will make every effort to resolve disputes amicably. If a resolution cannot be reached, parties agree to settle disputes through mediation or arbitration as permitted by local regulations.

11. Privacy Policy

  • Information collected during the service process is used solely for completing repairs and service communication.
  • Customer information will not be shared with third parties without consent.
